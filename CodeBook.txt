CodeBook
=================

The following variables from the Samsung data have been mean() summarized by activity class and test subject through the run_analysis.R script

 activity.nme - Name of the activity the subject was performing
 subject - The test subject related to the measurement. Range 1:30
 tBodyAcc.mean...X - Avg Body acceleration on X axis of phone
 tBodyAcc.mean...Y - Avg Body acceleration on Y axis of phone
 tBodyAcc.mean...Z - Avg Body acceleration on Z axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.mean...X - Avg Gravity acceleration on X axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.mean...Y - Avg Gravity acceleration on Y axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.mean...Z - Avg Gravity acceleration on Z axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.mean...X - Avg acceleration jerk of the body on X axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.mean...Y - Avg acceleration jerk of the body on Y axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.mean...Z - Avg acceleration jerk of the body on Z axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.mean...X - Avg Angular Velocity of Body on X axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.mean...Y - Avg Angular Velocity of Body on Y axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.mean...Z - Avg Angular Velocity of Body on Z axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.mean...X - Avg Angular Velocity Jerk of the Body on X axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.mean...Y - Avg Angular Velocity Jerk of the Body on Y axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.mean...Z - Avg Angular Velocity Jerk of the Body on Z axis of phone
 tBodyAccMag.mean.. - Avg estimated body acceleration magnitude
 tGravityAccMag.mean.. - Avg estimated gravity acceleration magnitude
 tBodyAccJerkMag.mean.. - Avg estimated jerk of body acceleration magnitude
 tBodyGyroMag.mean.. - Avg estimated angular velocity magnitued
 tBodyGyroJerkMag.mean.. - Avg estimated jerk of body angular velocity magnitude
 tBodyAcc.std...X - Avg standard deviation of body acceleration on X axis of phone
 tBodyAcc.std...Y - Avg standard deviation of body acceleration on Y axis of phone
 tBodyAcc.std...Z - Avg standard deviation of body acceleration on Z axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.std...X - Avg standard deviation of gravity acceleration on X axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.std...Y - Avg standard deviation of gravity acceleration on Y axis of phone
 tGravityAcc.std...Z - Avg standard deviation of gravity acceleration on Z axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.std...X - Avg standard deviation jerk of body acceleration on X axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.std...Y - Avg standard deviation jerk of body acceleration on Y axis of phone
 tBodyAccJerk.std...Z - Avg standard deviation jerk of body acceleration on Z axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.std...X - Avg standard deviation of angular velocity on X axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.std...Y - Avg standard deviation of angular velocity on Y axis of phone
 tBodyGyro.std...Z - Avg standard deviation of angular velocity on Z axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.std...X - Avg standard deviation jerk of body angular velocity on X axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.std...Y - Avg standard deviation jerk of body angular velocity on Y axis of phone
 tBodyGyroJerk.std...Z - Avg standard deviation jerk of body angular velocity on Z axis of phone
 tBodyAccMag.std.. - Avg standard deviation of estimated body acceleration magnitude
 tGravityAccMag.std.. - Avg standard deviation of estimated gravity acceleration magnitude
 tBodyAccJerkMag.std.. - Avg standard deviation of estimated jerk of body acceleration magnitude
 tBodyGyroMag.std.. - Avg standard deviation of estimated angular velocity magnitude
 tBodyGyroJerkMag.std.. - Avg standard deviation of estimated jerk of body angular velocity magnitude
